---
title: "Artist Verification"
---

Any staff with the kick perm can use the /artistverify command to verify artists after meeting all requirements.

### Artist Requirements

- 6 completed Art Pieces
- WIP of any of those completed arts (showing layers)
- Social media that you use to post artwork (Twitter, Furaffinity, Deviantart, etc)
- Commission TOS\
  (A TOS explains what the customer can expect. Please at the very least have a Refund policy and turnaround time. This needs to be accessible to your customers. DO NOT give screenshots of TOS, it needs to be posted on your social media.)
